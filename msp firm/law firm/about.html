About Us

Nepal's Biggest Law Firm

As nationally recognized lawyers with a reputation for securing some of the largest settlements and verdicts in Nepal. We don’t back down from a challenge.
A full service corporate and commercial law firm, MSPFirm is particularly known for assisting new market entrants in Nepal, especially in the country’s burgeoning technology sector. The team’s Kathmandu office is led by three partners: Shreetika Poudel, Prakriti Ojha and Monica Acharya.
Poudel is managing partner and is highly experienced in project finance, particularly in relation to hydropower. She also specialises in corporate, FDI and IP law.
Ojha has a similar focus on project finance and is also skilled in banking, aviation and telecoms work.
Acharya, meanwhile, focuses on FDI, labour cyber law and corporate work.
