Our Practice Areas

A full service corporate and commercial law firm,Imperial Law Associates is particularly known for assisting new market entrants in Nepal, especially in the country’s burgeoning technology sector. The team’s Kathmandu office is led by three partners: Nabin Bhandari, Surendra Tiwari and Deepesh Ojha. Bhandari is managing partner and is highly experienced in project finance, particularly in relation to hydropower. He also specialises in corporate, FDI and IP law. Ojha has a similar focus on project finance and is also skilled in banking, aviation and telecoms work. Tiwari, meanwhile, focuses on FDI, labour cyber law and corporate work.
